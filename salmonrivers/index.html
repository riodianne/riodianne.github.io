Rafting or white rafting is an extreme sport which uses an inflatable raft while paddling across a river or any body of water. It may sound dangerous, but don’t you worry, you have safety gears like helmet, life vest and of course your team (your friends) as you cross the rough water. Like any other sports, teamwork is very important! You and your team should be coordinated and be synchronized as you paddle. You will feel mixed emotions here, you will shout, scream, and feel the adrenaline rush but that’s part of the whole experience! This recreational and outdoor sports is perfect for you and your family!

The Bucket List

Many travellers are looking for extreme sports like white rafting to be added in their bucket list. As they do so, they will fell sense of satisfaction because they did something “extreme” and “extra-ordinary”! Mostly, these travellers ranges from twenty-forty (20-40) years old who are excited to explore nature!

Rafting or white rafting is ideal and perfect for sports enthusiasts, active, and adventurous people. Family and friends who are into extreme and outdoor sports may find this very interesting and enjoyable! White rafting does not require someone to be a swimmer (so don’t worry if you don’t know how to swim) as floating devices are provided.
